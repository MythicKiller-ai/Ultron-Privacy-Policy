Ultron™ Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 18 December 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Ultron™. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your data when you use our Discord bot.

2. Data Collection

Ultron™ may collect the following data to provide its services:

  • Discord user ID and username

  • Message content (for AFK, logging, economy, giveaways)
  • Bot usage statistics

3. Data Usage

  • Provide bot commands and features

4. Data Sharing

Ultron™ does not sell your data. Limited sharing may occur for commands like NQN, mass unban logs, or server logs as required for bot functionality.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect your data. Users are responsible for managing permissions and sharing within their servers.

6. User Control

You may:

  • Opt out of certain features
  • Request deletion of server data by contacting the bot developer

7. Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the bot constitutes acceptance of changes.

8. Contact

Questions? Contact the Ultron™ developer via Discord or the official support server.
